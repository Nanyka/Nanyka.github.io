Introduction

Being Inspired by playing some battle games is that it takes much time to play. As a result, one creative idea was chosen to create a battle game in a more straightforward way. At the beginning of the project, the game product is a simple graphics platform and is not reachable for customers’ interest in playing the game. The purpose of this game is to create entertaining and easy to understand for players having fun in leisure time that can be played with one finger and publish this game to some game channels. Just one finger to control characters in gameplay can bring customers having a highly entertaining time.Game Players manage their characters, movement, avoid trap systems, and attack other characters following to change the position character of each other and how to try keeping characters’ lives
